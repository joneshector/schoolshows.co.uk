Our Mission

At School Shows, we believe that theatre has the power to transform young lives. Our carefully crafted scripts are designed to engage students, build confidence, and create memorable experiences that last a lifetime.

With over 15 years of experience in educational theatre, we understand the unique challenges and opportunities that school productions present. Our scripts are specifically written to be accessible, engaging, and educationally valuable.

Our Approach

  • Educational Value: Every script incorporates learning opportunities across multiple subjects and skills.
  • Inclusive Casting: Flexible roles that accommodate diverse cast sizes and abilities.
  • Professional Quality: High-standard scripts that rival professional theatre productions.
  • Teacher Support: Comprehensive guides and resources to support educators throughout the production process.

Our Values

Passion for Theatre

We believe in the transformative power of live performance and its ability to inspire creativity and confidence in young people.

Educational Excellence

Every script is designed with educational outcomes in mind, supporting curriculum goals and personal development.

Teacher Support

We provide comprehensive resources and ongoing support to help educators create successful productions.
